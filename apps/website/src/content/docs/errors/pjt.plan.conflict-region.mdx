---
title: pjt.plan.conflict-region
slug: errors/pjt.plan.conflict-region
description: Two blueprints share the same ownerId for a region in one file.
sidebar:
  label: pjt.plan.conflict-region
---

**What**: Region ownership is keyed by `ownerId` (which is the blueprint's `id`). When two blueprints share an id and target the same file, the planner can't tell their regions apart.

**How to fix**: Give each blueprint a unique `id`. If you control both blueprints, namespace them (`my-org:gitignore:macos` vs `other-org:gitignore:macos`). If they're third-party, you can only have one of them in your `.pjt.ts` at a time.
